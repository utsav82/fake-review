1. E. F. Cardoso, R. M. Silva, and T. A. Almeida  
   "Towards automatic filtering of fake reviews", Neurocomputing, vol. 309, pp. 106-116, Oct. 2018.  
   - Focus: Proposes automatic filtering mechanisms for fake reviews using advanced computational techniques.  

2. Y. Ren and Y. Zhang  
   "Deceptive opinion spam detection using neural network", Proc. 26th Int. Conf. Comput. Linguistics: Tech. Papers (COLING), pp. 140-150, 2016.  
   - Focus: Neural network-based detection of opinion spam in reviews.  

3. N. Jindal and B. Liu  
   "Opinion spam and analysis", Proc. Int. Conf. Web Search Web Data Mining (WSDM), pp. 219-230, 2008.  
   - Focus: One of the pioneering works in opinion spam and fake review detection.  

4. A. Heydari, M. Tavakoli, and N. Salim  
   "Detection of fake opinions using time series", Expert Syst. Appl., vol. 58, pp. 83-92, Oct. 2016.  
   - Focus: Time-series approaches for detecting fake reviews.  

5. H. Aghakhani, A. Machiry, S. Nilizadeh, C. Kruegel, and G. Vigna  
   "Detecting deceptive reviews using generative adversarial networks", Proc. IEEE Secur. Privacy Workshops (SPW), pp. 89-95, May 2018.  
   - Focus: Utilizes GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks) for identifying deceptive reviews.  

6. A. Mukherjee, V. Venkataraman, B. Liu, and N. Glance 
   "Fake review detection: Classification and analysis of real and pseudo reviews", 2013.  
   - Focus: Analysis and classification of fake and real reviews in e-commerce.
